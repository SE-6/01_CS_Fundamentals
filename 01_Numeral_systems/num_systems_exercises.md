## Exercises

### Decimal to Binary:

- Convert 25 (decimal) to binary.
  25 / 2 = 12 r 1
  12 / 2 = 6 r 0
  6 / 2 = 3 r 0
  3 / 2 = 1 r 1
  1 / 2 = 0 r 1

--> 0b11001

32 16 8 4 2 1
0 1 1 0 0 1

- Convert 100 (decimal) to binary.

- Convert 47 (decimal) to binary.
  47 / 2 = 23 r 1
  23 / 2 = 11 r 1
  11 / 2 = 5 r 1
  5 / 2 = 2 r 1
  2 / 2 = 1 r 0
  1 / 2 = 0 r 1

-> 0b101111

### Binary to Decimal:

- Convert 101101 (binary) to decimal.

  32 16 8 4 2 1
  1 0 1 1 0 1
  32 + 8 + 4 + 1 = 45

- Convert 1110001 (binary) to decimal.

- Convert 10010 (binary) to decimal.

  16 8 4 2 1
  1 0 0 1 0
  16 + 2 = 18

### Decimal to Hexadecimal:

- Convert 255 (decimal) to hexadecimal.

  255 // 16 = 15 r 15 -> f
  15 // 16 = 0 r 15 -> f

-> 0xff

- Convert 64 (decimal) to hexadecimal.

- Convert 202 (decimal) to hexadecimal.
  202 / 16 = 12 r 10 -> a
  12 / 16 = 0 r 12 -> c

-> 0xca

### Hexadecimal to Decimal:

- Convert A3 (hex) to decimal.
  a -> 10
  10 _ 16 = 160
  3 _ 1
  163

- Convert 1F (hex) to decimal.
  16**0 = 1
  1 _ f = 1 _ 15 = 15
  16**1 = 16
  16\*1 = 16
  15 + 16 = 31

- Convert C9 (hex) to decimal.

### Binary to Hexadecimal (and vice versa):

- Convert 1101_1110 (binary) to hexadecimal.

1110 -> 14 -> e
1101 -> 13 -> d

-> 0xde

- Convert 7B (hex) to binary.
  7 -> 0111
  B (11) -> 1011

7B -> 0b0111_1011

- Convert 1001_0110 (binary) to hexadecimal.
  1001 -> 8 + 1 = 9
  0110 -> 4 + 2 = 6

-> 0x96
